The Score

A Great War Requiem
interactive score

Seven movements · SATB choir and organ · approximately forty minutes

A Great War Requiem is a seven-movement setting for SATB choir and organ. Each movement pairs a traditional Requiem Mass plainchant with a poem by one of four women of the Great War — Vera Brittain, Winifred M. Letts, Margaret Cole, or Josephine Preston Peabody — weaving voices silenced by history into the Requiem's liturgical frame.
